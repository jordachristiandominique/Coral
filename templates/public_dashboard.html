What do these numbers mean?

Class A - High coral coverage means live coral covers at least 60% of the survey points, and generally indicates strong reef condition. Class B - Moderate coral coverage (40-59%) reflects moderate health with stable recovery potential. Class C - Low coral coverage (below 40%) signals a reef area that needs closer monitoring and targeted restoration support.
